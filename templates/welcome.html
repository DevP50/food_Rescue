How NoWasteAm Works

Three steps.
Zero waste.

01
🏪
Restaurant Lists Surplus

At end of service, a restaurant posts what's left — meals, bread, produce — with a pickup window. Takes under a minute. No waste, no guilt.

02
📍
You Get the Alert

Nearby users get an instant notification. Browse what's available, claim your portion, and get directions — before someone else does.

03
🤝
Pick Up & Pay Forward

Collect your food at no or low cost before closing. Every rescue shows up on a live community impact board — meals saved, CO₂ avoided, people fed.
